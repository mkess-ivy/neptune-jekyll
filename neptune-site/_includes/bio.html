Many creators find peace in their art. Neptune, a 23 year old poet & spoken word artist from Washington, DC, has done just that. Finding her niche in developing thought­ provoking messages to uplift and inform, Neptune consistently inspires her community. With multiple service projects under her belt, it is evident that she is not just about talk but action. “Let’s Evolve Together” is the driving mantra of her work, as it encompasses her investment in compassionate community building. Her words and activism have been featured in the Baltimore Sun and other local press.
